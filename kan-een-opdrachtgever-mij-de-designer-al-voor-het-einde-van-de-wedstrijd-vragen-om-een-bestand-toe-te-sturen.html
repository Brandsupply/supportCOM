Can a client ask me, the designer, to send a file before the contest ends?

Sometimes, a client may ask a designer to send the design file before the contest ends.

Of course, it's up to the designer to decide whether to agree to this.

However, the payment has not yet been made, and therefore, the transfer of copyright has not taken place either.

To avoid someone using your design without permission and without payment, our advice is not to send files prematurely. Keep communicating within the Brandsupply contest platform while the contest is ongoing.

Once the client has selected a winner, we will ask the designer to upload the files via a link. This way, we can monitor the file transfer, ensure the prize money is paid, and prevent any issues with copyright.
