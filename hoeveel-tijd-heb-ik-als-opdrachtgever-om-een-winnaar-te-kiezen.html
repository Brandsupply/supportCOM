How much time do I have as a client to choose a winner?

The client selects the duration of the contest at the start. Once the contest has ended, it's time to choose a winner. We ask the client to select a winner within one week.

Most clients can decide quickly, as they have followed the contest closely and provided feedback to the designers. By the end of the contest, it’s usually clear which designs are the favorites. Some clients may need a bit more time, for example, to consult with others.

If the client isn’t 100% convinced by the submissions when the contest ends, they can extend the contest for one more week.

However, the client is not obligated to choose a winner and can close the contest without selecting one if none of the designs are suitable. We trust in our designers, and this happens very rarely.

The client is required to choose a winner if they selected a guaranteed contest.
