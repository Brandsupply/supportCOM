How and when does the designer receive the prize?

Once the client has selected a design as the winner, the designer will automatically receive a notification with a link to upload the files. Once the client has approved these documents, Brandsupply will make the payment.

Brandsupply transfers the prize money via electronic bank transfer to the winning designers. Prize payments are made every Tuesday (except on official holidays).

If the designer does not have an IBAN, Brandsupply can also pay via PayPal. In this case, the designer should enter their PayPal address in the 'IBAN' field in MyBrandsupply.

It's important for designers to keep their payment details up to date in MyBrandsupply!
