Originality and copyright, guidelines for designers.

To ensure fair contests and original designs, Brandsupply has established several guidelines regarding copyright and originality of submissions:

  1. Submit designs that are entirely created by you.
  2. Do not use stock material.
  3. Do not copy from other designers participating in the contest. Of course, listen to the client's preferences (e.g., color, shape, etc.), but make sure to submit an original idea!
  4. Remain polite and do not publicly accuse others.
  5. If you believe someone is violating the rules, click the 'report' button on the design. Explain to us why you think the rules are being violated in that way.
  6. Brandsupply closely monitors the reports and makes decisions as an independent party regarding the submitted reports.
  7. You can also directly contact our customer service.

Brandsupply can deny access to the platform for any designer who violates these rules.
